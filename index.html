SpaceR

A simple project that improves learning of the normal flashcard technique using spaced repetition. What's spaced-repetition? It's a technique that exploits the natural tendency of the brain to continue to process information after having seen it. Recalling information (like words) at diminishing intervals as you get better at understanding improves the overall rate at which you learn. I refactored a shell of this project to implement the spaced repetition algorithm using a linked list and helped architect the front end interface with my friend Jon.

JQuery, vanilla Javascript, a11y approved HTML 5 & CSS.
